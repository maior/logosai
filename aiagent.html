Frequently Asked Questions

How do I switch between themes?
You can easily switch themes by setting the mode parameter in your configuration. The widget will automatically adjust its appearance to match your selected theme.
Can I customize the widget's position?
Yes! You can position the widget anywhere on your page using the position configuration. Simply adjust the bottom, right, top, or left values to place it exactly where you want it.
Is it responsive on mobile devices?
Absolutely! The widget is fully responsive and automatically adjusts its size and layout for optimal viewing on all devices, from desktop to mobile.
